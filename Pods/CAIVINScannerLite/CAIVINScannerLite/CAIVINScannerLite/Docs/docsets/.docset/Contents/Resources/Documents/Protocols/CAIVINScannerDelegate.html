CAIVINScannerDelegate

public protocol CAIVINScannerDelegate

The CoxAutomotiveVINScannerDelegate protocol is adopted by an object or type to receive information about the VIN scanning process. This information is also provided via the use of completion handlers.

Messages related to the scanner’s user iterface are also sent to the delegate , allowing you to provide your own implementations for handling help and keyboard display requests.

    A scan has be completed. This does not mean that the scan was successful, only that scanner completed the process of scanning a VIN.
